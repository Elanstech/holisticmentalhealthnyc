Individual Therapy

Personalized one-on-one support for your mental health journey

What to Expect

Individual therapy provides a confidential, safe space to explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Sessions are tailored to your unique needs and goals, focusing on personal growth and healing.

  • Session Length: 50 minutes
  • Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly
  • Approach: Personalized treatment plan
  • Methods: Evidence-based therapeutic techniques
  • Progress: Regular assessments and adjustments

Before Treatment

Preparing for therapy helps maximize the benefits of your sessions:

  • Free Consultation: 15-minute phone call to discuss your needs
  • Intake Assessment: Comprehensive evaluation during first session
  • Goal Setting: Collaboratively identify specific areas to address
  • Insurance Verification: We'll help verify coverage and discuss payment
  • Paperwork: Complete intake forms before your first session

During Treatment

What happens in your therapy sessions:

  • Safe Environment: Non-judgmental space to share openly
  • Practical Tools: Learn coping strategies for daily use
  • Emotional Processing: Work through difficult emotions
  • Pattern Recognition: Develop insights into recurring thoughts
  • Skill Building: Build resilience and emotional regulation

After Treatment

Long-term benefits and continued growth:

  • Improved Wellbeing: Greater emotional stability
  • Coping Skills: Effective tools to manage challenges
  • Self-Awareness: Deeper understanding of yourself
  • Better Relationships: Enhanced communication with others
  • Maintenance Plan: Strategies to maintain progress

Couples Counseling

Strengthen your relationship and deepen your connection

What to Expect

Couples counseling helps partners improve communication, resolve conflicts, and build a stronger, healthier relationship.

  • Session Length: 60 minutes
  • Frequency: Weekly sessions recommended
  • Approach: Evidence-based couples therapy
  • Methods: Gottman Method, EFT, Communication Training

Before Treatment

  • Joint Consultation: Both partners meet with therapist
  • Relationship Assessment: Comprehensive evaluation of dynamics
  • Commitment Agreement: Both partners commit to the process
  • Goal Alignment: Define shared objectives

During Treatment

  • Communication Skills: Learn effective listening techniques
  • Conflict Resolution: Address disagreements constructively
  • Trust Building: Rebuild emotional safety
  • Intimacy Enhancement: Deepen emotional connection

Mindfulness-Based Therapy

Find peace through present-moment awareness

What to Expect

Mindfulness-based therapy integrates meditation and awareness practices with traditional therapy to reduce stress and enhance wellbeing.

  • Session Length: 50-60 minutes
  • Approach: MBSR, MBCT, DBT mindfulness

During Treatment

  • Breath Work: Practice breathing exercises
  • Body Awareness: Develop connection to physical sensations
  • Present-Moment Focus: Train your mind to stay in the now

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Evidence-based treatment for lasting change

What to Expect

CBT is a structured, goal-oriented approach that helps you identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors.

  • Session Length: 50 minutes
  • Duration: Typically 12-20 sessions
  • Approach: Structured, goal-focused treatment

During Treatment

  • Thought Identification: Recognize automatic negative thoughts
  • Cognitive Restructuring: Challenge distorted thinking
  • Behavioral Activation: Increase positive activities
  • Skill Practice: Learn and practice coping strategies

Group Therapy

Healing through shared experiences and community support

What to Expect

Group therapy brings together people with similar challenges in a supportive, confidential environment led by a trained therapist.

  • Session Length: 90 minutes
  • Group Size: 6-10 participants
  • Format: Structured topics and open discussion

During Treatment

  • Peer Support: Connect with others who understand
  • Shared Experiences: Learn you're not alone
  • Multiple Perspectives: Gain insights from diverse viewpoints
  • Skill Practice: Practice new behaviors in safe environment
