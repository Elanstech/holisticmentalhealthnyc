Individual Therapy

Personalized one-on-one support for your mental health journey

What to Expect

Individual therapy provides a confidential, safe space to explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. Sessions are tailored to your unique needs and goals, focusing on personal growth and healing.

  • Session Length: 50 minutes
  • Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly
  • Approach: Personalized treatment plan
  • Methods: Evidence-based therapeutic techniques
  • Progress: Regular assessments and adjustments

Before Treatment

Preparing for therapy helps maximize the benefits of your sessions:

  • Free Consultation: 15-minute phone call to discuss your needs
  • Intake Assessment: Comprehensive evaluation during first session
  • Goal Setting: Collaboratively identify specific areas to address
  • Insurance Verification: We'll help verify coverage and discuss payment
  • Paperwork: Complete intake forms before your first session

During Treatment

What happens in your therapy sessions:

  • Safe Environment: Non-judgmental space to share openly
  • Practical Tools: Learn coping strategies for daily use
  • Emotional Processing: Work through difficult emotions
  • Pattern Recognition: Develop insights into recurring thoughts
  • Skill Building: Build resilience and emotional regulation

After Treatment

Long-term benefits and continued growth:

  • Improved Wellbeing: Greater emotional stability
  • Coping Skills: Effective tools to manage challenges
  • Self-Awareness: Deeper understanding of yourself
  • Better Relationships: Enhanced communication with others
  • Maintenance Plan: Strategies to maintain progress

Your first session, often called an "intake," is designed to help us understand you and your needs:

  • Introduction: Get to know your therapist and establish comfort
  • Assessment: Discuss your concerns, history, and current situation
  • Goal Setting: Identify what you hope to achieve through therapy
  • Treatment Plan: Collaboratively develop an approach tailored to you
  • Questions: Ask anything about the therapy process

First sessions typically last 50-60 minutes. Remember, therapy is a collaborative process, and your therapist is there to support you without judgment.

The duration of therapy varies greatly depending on your individual needs and goals:

  • Short-term therapy: 8-12 sessions for specific issues or situations
  • Medium-term therapy: 3-6 months for moderate concerns
  • Long-term therapy: 6+ months for deep-rooted issues or ongoing support

Many clients see improvement within the first few sessions. We'll regularly review your progress together and adjust your treatment plan as needed. You're in control of how long you continue therapy.

We believe everyone deserves access to quality mental health care. If you don't have insurance:

  • Payment Plans: Flexible payment arrangements can be discussed
  • Superbills: We can provide documentation for out-of-network reimbursement

Please contact our office to discuss your situation. We're committed to working with you to find a solution that makes therapy accessible.

Yes, confidentiality is a cornerstone of therapy. Everything you share in sessions is protected by law and professional ethics. We take your privacy very seriously.

Limited exceptions include:

  • Risk of harm to yourself or others
  • Suspected child or elder abuse
  • Court order requiring disclosure

Your therapist will discuss these limits in your first session. Outside these rare circumstances, your information remains completely private and will not be shared without your written consent.

EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) is a specialized, evidence-based therapy for processing traumatic memories and reducing their emotional impact.

EMDR can help with:

  • PTSD and trauma
  • Anxiety and panic disorders
  • Depression
  • Phobias
  • Grief and loss
  • Performance anxiety

Dr. Kristie Doheny is EMDR certified and has extensive experience using this powerful technique to help clients heal from past trauma and move forward in their lives.

Finding the right therapist is crucial for successful therapy. We make this process easy:

  • Free Consultation: Discuss your needs with our intake coordinator
  • Therapist Matching: We'll recommend therapists based on your concerns and preferences
  • Specialization: Each therapist has unique expertise (trauma, couples, anxiety, etc.)
  • Meet & Greet: Your first session is a chance to assess fit
  • Flexibility: You can request a different therapist if needed

The therapeutic relationship is key to success. We want you to feel comfortable and supported from day one.
